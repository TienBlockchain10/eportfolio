03 : About me

Blockchain Student Society

I am one of the Co-Founders and current president of Blockchain Student Society. What started as an idea in the kitchen grew to over 350+ members in under a year. Our aim of this non-profit is to bring FREE educational resources, seminars, and provide a community for the crypto-curious.

Crypto, Blockchain, and Web3

I have been in the crypto space for over 2 years now, and have delved into all the aspects of it including Decentralized Finance, Cross Chain Bridges, Smart Contract Development on EVM compatible chains.

From doing research on emerging technologies for the National Association of Realtors, to piloting a Blockchain 101 course at Lamar University, I love to explore this ever changing space and will continue to dive deeper into it.
